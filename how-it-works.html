Step One

Design —
Get to know you.

Before anything gets built, we take the time to understand you and your business — what you need, what you like, and where you're starting from.

Already have a website?

Tweaks or a fresh start?

We'll look at what you've got and figure out the best direction — whether that's polishing what's there or starting over with something much better.

  • Are you after minor touch-ups, or a complete redesign?
  • What's working on your current site — and what isn't?
  • Tell us about your business — what do you do, who do you serve?
or

No website yet?

Let's build something new.

Starting from scratch is actually the easiest path. We ask a few simple questions to understand your taste and your business — and go from there.

  • Do you already have a logo?
  • Any websites you like the look or feel of?
  • Tell us about your business — what do you offer and who's it for?
02

Step Two

Develop —
Building to preference.

How we build depends on where you're starting from. Here's the honest difference between the two paths.

Existing website

Working with what's there.

Altering a site someone else built is a bit like renovating a house you've never lived in. We have to understand the existing structure first — how it was put together, what each part does — before we can safely change or add to it without things breaking or looking out of place.

It's absolutely doable, but it takes a little more care and time. We'll always be upfront with you about that.

vs

Brand new website

Designing from the ground up.

Building fresh is the cleanest way to work — like designing a new home from scratch. We're not constrained by anyone else's decisions. Everything is built around your brand, your content, and your goals.

A logo, a colour preference, or even just a couple of sites you like the look of gives us everything we need to get started.

03

Step Three

Completion —
You're ready to go live.

When the build is signed off, we don't just hand you files and disappear. We make sure you understand what you've got and feel confident going forward.

🌐

Domain

Your web address — how people find you online. We walk you through what it is, how it points to your site, and how to manage it yourself if needed.

🔒

Security

SSL, HTTPS, and keeping your site protected. We explain what's in place and what it means — no jargon, just plain English.

☁️

Hosting

Where your website actually lives on the internet. We cover your options whether you're managing it yourself or staying with us on a managed plan.

If you choose to take the site as a one-off and run it yourself — that's completely fine. We want you to feel confident doing that. We won't leave you in the dark just because you're not staying on a plan.
