FAQ

Questions we
always get asked.

Can I switch from Solo to Managed later?

Yes, absolutely. If you start on a solo build and later decide you'd like us to host and manage it, we can migrate your site onto our platform at any time. There's a small one-off migration fee, then you're on the monthly plan from there.

Do I need to provide my own content?

Not necessarily. If you have copy and images ready, great — we'll use them. If not, we can write basic page content and source royalty-free images for you at no extra charge.

How long does it actually take?

Most websites are done in under 2 weeks from the moment we receive your content and brief. Complex e-commerce builds may take a little longer, but we'll give you a realistic timeline upfront.

What exactly is included in the managed plan?

Hosting, security monitoring, SSL, content updates, monthly performance reports, and priority support. See our Services page for a full tier breakdown with pricing.

Is there a contract on the managed plan?

No. You can cancel any time with one month's notice. We don't believe in locking people in — if you're not happy, you should be free to leave.

How much does a website cost?

Solo builds start from £299 one-off. Managed plans start from £35/month (which includes a reduced build fee). Head to our Services page for the full breakdown.
